• Assumptions considered:
  1. Independence
     Observations are independent between and within groups (male and female students are different individuals).
  2. Binary outcome
     The response variable is binary (0= non-smoker vs 1= current smoker).
  3. Large sample condition
     Both groups satisfy the large sample conditions of np ≥ 10 and n(1−p) ≥ 10, ensuring the validity of the normal approximation.
  4. Random sampling assumption
     The YRBS dataset is based on a survey sampling design and is treated as approximately representative of the population.
